Event

These are trophies that were awarded to teams for their performance in official Overcast events.

Title Description Icon
[Tournament] Win the [Tournament Name] Tournament
[Tournament] 2nd Place 2nd Place in the [Tournament Name] Tournament
[Tournament] 3rd Place 3rd Place in the [Tournament Name] Tournament
[Mini Event] Win [Mini Event Name]

These trophies are given out to the winning team of each tournament or event. It is possible for players to have more than one of each trophy, and its title and description is dependant on the name of the event. For example, winners of the Conquest tournament will have their trophy called "Conquest".
